RECYCLING

This is the process of converting waste materials into new materials and objects. It is an alternative to "conventional" waste disposal that can save material and help lower greenhouse gas emissions. Recycling can prevent the waste of potentially useful materials and reduce the consumption of fresh raw materials, thereby reducing: energy usage, air pollution (from incineration), and water pollution (from landfilling). Recycling is a key component of modern waste reduction and is the third component of the "Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle" waste hierarchy.Thus, recycling aims at environmental sustainability by substituting raw material inputs into and redirecting waste outputs out of the economic system

The Online recyclable waste management system

ORWMS sole purpose of conception was to improve garbage management in the count oof Mombasa in the sense that the system has three main modules: The Collectors, |The Recycling Company, |The admin module, that works independently and only accessible to respective group levels. The collectors register into the system, post a recyling material offers (which they have collected in their location) and then posts them for recycling companies eho are registered with the system can view the offer and purchase them according to their category of processing. The admin is responsible for for managing both the collector and the recyler and also generate system report.
